Case-study standard

What each project must prove.

01

Problem

What practical, scientific, educational, or commercial issue required work?

02

Role

What did you personally plan, produce, analyze, manage, or deliver?

03

Method

Which experimental, analytical, technical, design, or operational methods were used?

04

Output

What code, website, course, figure, content, product system, or report was produced?

05

Evidence

Use public links, authorized images, analytics, or publication records. Do not invent metrics.

06

Limitations

State confidentiality, incomplete validation, pending publication, or missing performance data directly.
