Publication standard

Every image needs provenance.

01

Ownership

State whether the item was produced independently, for a client, for a university project, or as part of a publication.

02

Contribution

Specify concept, illustration, data visualization, editing, animation, copywriting, or full production.

03

AI disclosure

Disclose generative-AI assistance where it materially contributed to imagery, editing, or composition.

04

Permission

Confirm journal, institutional, client, participant, and copyright conditions before public display.
