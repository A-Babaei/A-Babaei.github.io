Research architecture

From lesion model to interpretable behavior.

01 / Question

Can PF stimulation modulate multidomain behavior?

The project examines stimulation-dependent changes across forelimb use, rotations, locomotion, rearing, immobility, grooming, and head-only movement.

02 / Experimental

Controlled rodent workflow

Unilateral lesion model, validation before implantation, defined stereotaxic targeting, parameterized DBS, and standardized behavioral testing.

03 / Analysis

Repeated-measures statistics

Count and duration outcomes require distributions and correlation structures appropriate to repeated observations rather than simple independent tests.

04 / Contribution

End-to-end integration

Experimental execution, video-scoring workflow, data preparation, statistical modeling, visualization, and manuscript development.

05 / Integrity

Explicit uncertainty

Model limitations, small-sample constraints, sex restriction, stimulation design, and pending validations must be stated rather than hidden.

06 / Outputs

Manuscript, code, figures

Publication links, datasets, repositories, and histological outputs should be added only after verification and public release.
